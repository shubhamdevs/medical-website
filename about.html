About US

Approach The Best Psychologist in Kolkata:

Working with you

As a psychologist Peace of Mon approach begins with an attitude of warmth, responsiveness, and kindness. We use collaborative inquiry to help clients to gain a greater understanding and insight into their problems. If you are searching for a "psychologist near me" in Kolkata, Peace of Mon may be a good option.

Every journey is different

Peace of Mon is very aware that each client’s journey through counselling is particular to them and very much unique. We may have a sense of being alone, feel hopeless, guilty or overwhelmed, but we might find it hard to pinpoint what in particular brought us here. Alternatively we may know exactly why we are here, but not have a clue what to do about it.

Tailored to your needs

Peace of Mon approach recognises how different each person’s experience is and addresses this by taking an integrative approach to counselling. Drawing on expertise in humanistic, psychodynamic and cognitive behavioural therapies, Peace of Mon tailors therapy to clients’ unique needs and requirements.
